Privacy Policy - Jagrati

Last Updated: October 2025

Publisher: Hemang Mishra

Jagrati (“we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your data when using our Android application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

  • Profile Photo: Captured using the device camera and stored securely on ImageKit. Can be updated or deleted by you anytime.
  • Google Account Information: Basic details used for Google Sign-In authentication only.
  • Device Token (from FCM): Used for sending notifications.

2. Why We Collect Data

  • To allow login and verify your identity.
  • To show your profile photo within the app.
  • To send important notifications.

3. Permissions Used

Camera: Required only when uploading/changing your profile photo. The camera is not used in the background.

4. User Control & Deletion

  • You may delete or change your profile photo anytime — old data is permanently removed from ImageKit.
  • You may delete your entire account via Settings → Account Deletion.
  • We comply with user-initiated data removal requests.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Google Sign-In
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging
  • ImageKit

6. Security

All communication is encrypted (HTTPS). Data is protected using secure access practices.
